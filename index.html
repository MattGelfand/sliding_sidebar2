Hybrid UX Design / Front-End Dev.

I'm a UX designer with front-end chops raised in New Jersey residing in Boston who doesn't talk about himself in the third person. A writer in a former life, I recently earned a master's degree in interctive publishing from this fine institution. I cut my teeth at a software lab helping to design apps to make journalists' lives easier, and shifted my focus to UX at an agency that's been a pioneer of the discipline for over a decade. I have a passion for manipulating the web and believe in the philosophy of building hastily and failing early, because your best idea is never your first. Other mantras: Steal like an artist, don't make users think, and always have a plan - without one you have no destination, and you can never arrive.
